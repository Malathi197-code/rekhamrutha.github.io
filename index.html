About Us

Rekhamrutha Mahila Mandali is a grassroots NGO dedicated to empowering women and uplifting rural communities through skill development, education, and social awareness. Based in Anantapuramu, we focus on real change through community-led action.

Our Programs

  • Self-Help Groups & Microfinance Training
  • Vocational Skills for Women & Youth
  • Health and Sanitation Awareness Camps
  • Education Support for Girl Children
  • Gender Equality & Legal Rights Workshops

Our Impact

Over 2,000 women trained, 1,500+ families impacted, and growing partnerships with local institutions and CSR donors.
