About Us

Rekhamrutha Mahila Mandali is a grassroots non-profit organization based in Anantapuramu, Andhra Pradesh, committed to empowering women, children, and marginalized communities through sustainable development. Founded with a vision to build self-reliant rural societies, the NGO works across sectors such as women‚Äôs entrepreneurship, health awareness, girl child education, skill development, and social justice.

We have impacted over 2,000 women and 1,500 families through initiatives like self-help group formation, vocational training, legal rights workshops, and rural health campaigns. Driven by compassion and a commitment to change, Rekhamrutha continues to partner with government schemes, CSR programs, and citizen donors to create a more equal and empowered India.

Major Projects

Vocational Skill Training

We offer sewing, tailoring, computer literacy, and craft training programs to help women and young girls gain financial independence and employable skills.

Health & Hygiene Awareness Camps

Through community camps in villages, we provide awareness and access to menstrual health, maternal care, and general hygiene practices for women and adolescent girls.

SHG Formation & Legal Literacy

We help rural women organize into self-help groups and conduct legal rights workshops to ensure they are aware of their entitlements and can access welfare programs.
